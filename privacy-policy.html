Privacy Policy

SOS Offline respects your privacy. We only use permissions like SMS, Location, and Call to provide SOS emergency features. No personal data is collected or shared.

Permissions Used

  • SMS: To send emergency messages to contacts.
  • Location: To attach your live location to SOS alerts.
  • Call: To automatically call emergency contacts.

We do not store or share any data outside your device.
